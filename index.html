The method

A diagnostic-first path to production.

Most AI projects die in the gap between demo and deployment. Ours are built backwards from that gap.

PHASE 01

Two weeks shadowing the work. We quantify the bottleneck and pick the one process with the steepest payoff.

PHASE 02

Prove

A working proof against your real data — in front of real users — so the business case is evidence, not optimism.

PHASE 03

Ship

We harden it into production: monitoring, guardrails, fallbacks, and a cost model that holds at scale.

PHASE 04

Hand off

Your team owns it. Documentation, training, and a 90-day improvement loop baked in before we step back.
